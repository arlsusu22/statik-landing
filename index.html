Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction
Statik ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application uses your data when you connect it with the Strava platform.

2. Data Collection and Usage
Statik uses the Strava API to retrieve your activity data (maps, distance, time) solely for the purpose of generating visual overlays on your device.

We do not permanently store your Strava activity data on our servers. All data processing happens temporarily to generate the image you requested.

3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data remains strictly between Strava and your device.

4. User Consent & Permissions
By connecting your Strava account, you grant Statik permission to access activity data required for visualization. You retain full control over which data is shared via the Strava authentication screen (e.g., Public vs. Private activities). Statik only processes data you have explicitly authorized us to view.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@appstatik.com
