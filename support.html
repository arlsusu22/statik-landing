Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase? Go to the paywall screen inside the app and tap the "Restore Purchases" button at the bottom.
How do I cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open your iPhone Settings > Tap your name > Subscriptions > Statik > Cancel Subscription.
My activity isn't syncing? Please ensure you have granted Statik permission to access your Strava data. You can re-connect in the app settings.
